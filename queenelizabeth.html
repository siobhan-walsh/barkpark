Queen Elizabeth- Cambie Street

3/5 paws

Queen elizabeth park is quite nice with a big field and tennis courts. Be careful if it’s been raining recently because this park can get muddy. Also, it may not be best for dogs who wonder, as it is very close to the road, with no fence.
