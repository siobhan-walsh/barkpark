Warner Loat- Winston Street

5/5 paws

Warner Loat is a wonderful dog park in Burnaby. It has a big fully fenced, off-leash area, as well as logs, and other obstacles for dogs to play on. There is a water station for thirsty dogs, and plenty of parking area.
