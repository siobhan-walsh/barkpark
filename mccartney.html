McCartney Creek Park

Larkhall Crescent


4/5 paws

Amenities:

McCartney Creek Park is located in the Blueridge area of North Vancouver and is a very popular dog hangout. It has two fields, trails, tennis courts and a creek. Dog owners often take their dogs through the trails and play fetch on the fields. The main drawback is that the main field is available for dogs during baseball season, however there is another field that dogs can go to.
